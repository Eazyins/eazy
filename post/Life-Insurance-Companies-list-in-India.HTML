Life Insurance Companies list in India

List of Life Insurance Companies in India, Life Insurance Company List, Life Insurance Company Names, Life Insurance Company Information

There are many life insurance companies in India, which provide life insurance plans. Life insurance is an agreement between an individual and an insurance company under which the insurance company pays death benefits to the insured's family in case of unfortunate death of the insured during the policy term. Before buying a life insurance policy, you should find an insurance company that you can trust completely.

Life Insurance Companies list in India

Life Insurance Corporation (LIC) India Company
Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) is the oldest insurance company in our country. LIC was established on 1 September 1956, when the Parliament of India passed the Life Insurance Act of India which nationalized the insurance industry in India. LIC offers a variety of insurance products to its customers.
Some of the common products offered by the company are Life Insurance Plans, Pension Plans, Unit Linked Plans, Micro Insurance Plans, Group Schemes, etc. With a network of 2,048 branches, the company has a large number of employees working in various cities and towns across the country.
Tata AIA Life Insurance Company
This life insurance company was launched in the year 2001 as a joint venture between AIA Group Limited and Tata Sons Private Limited. The majority stake in this joint venture is 75% held by Tata Sons and 25% by AIA Group of Companies.
Tata AIA follows a completely customer-centric approach and offers a wide range of life insurance. Make plans to meet the future needs of your policyholders. This company offers many insurance plans like group plans, child plans, wealth plans, protection plans, savings plans, and micro-insurance plans.
SBI Life Insurance Company
SBI Life Insurance is an Indian life insurance company. This insurance company was started in March 2001 as a joint venture with the State Bank of India (SBI) and BNP Paribas Cardiff. SBI Life Insurance Company provides life insurance and pension products at very affordable rates. SBI Life Insurance has a network of more than 950 branches.
Max Life Insurance Company
Max Financial Services Limited and Mitsui Sumitomo Insurance Company Limited jointly launched Max Life Insurance. The company offers long-term protection, savings, child insurance, and retirement schemes with multi-channel distribution partners and highly serviced agencies. All the insurance plans offered by Max Life are loaded with benefits and various essential features as per the needs of the individual.
Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance
Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance Company was started in 2001 as a joint venture between European financial services company Allianz SE and Bajaj Finserv Ltd.
Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance Company offers term insurance plans, ULIP plans, savings plans, investment plans, child insurance plans, and retirement plans. The company offers a vast range of customized products, which meet every single demand of the customer and provide them with a transparent benefit.
HDFC Life Insurance Company
HDFC Life Insurance Company is a life insurance company. This company was incorporated by a Joint Venture between Standard Life Aberdeen plc and Housing Development Finance Corporation Limited. This insurance company was started in the year 2000 and its headquarter is located in Mumbai, Maharashtra. This company provides various types of life insurance plans to its customers like Suraksha plan, Pension plan, Mahila plan, Child plan, Health plan, etc. This life insurance company offers several insurance policies specially designed for children and women. The policyholder can add additional riders to the plans to increase the coverage.
ICICI Prudential Life Insurance
ICICI Prudential Life Insurance Company is a life insurance company in India. The company was set up by a joint venture between ICICI Bank and Prudential Corporation Holdings Limited. ICICI Prudential Life Insurance is one of the best life insurance companies in India. It was included in the list of top life insurance companies in 2019.
To meet the living requirements of the customers, ICICI Prudential Life Insurance offers a wide range of products that enable buyers to achieve long-term goals. ICICI Prudential Life Insurance offers products such as term plans, ULIP plans, pension plans, child plans, and investment plans.
Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance
Aditya Birla Sun Life Insurance Company Limited was incorporated by the Joint Venture of Aditya Birla Group and Sun Life Financial INC. This life insurance company has more than 600 branches in more than 500 cities across the country.
This life insurance company offers many life insurance plans to its customers. These include children's plans, Dhan Suraksha plans, retirement and pension solutions, health plans, term insurance plans, Unit Linked Insurance Plans (ULIPs), etc.
