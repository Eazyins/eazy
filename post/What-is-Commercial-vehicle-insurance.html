What is Commercial vehicle insurance

This motor insurance policy is for business vehicles and can be customized to suit your needs. Commercial vehicle insurance covers any damage and loss caused to or caused by commercial vehicles. This includes damage to vehicles due to an accident, sabotage, natural causes, and fire. All businesses must buy commercial vehicle insurance for all their commercial vehicles such as auto-rickshaws, cabs, school buses, and trucks.

Why do you need commercial vehicle insurance?


If you are buying and using commercial vehicles in your business, then you need to buy commercial vehicle insurance that will cover the financial loss and losses caused to your business due to the vehicle and the people driving the vehicle. can do.

If your main business is related to vehicles, such as you provide taxi service or drive a private school bus, then a commercial vehicle insurance plan will cover your financial loss in case of any mishap as well as the people sitting in that business and those in that business. It also covers losses to those who put money.

By law, all commercial vehicle owners must have at least one third-party liability-only policy for each commercial vehicle, which can cover any damages caused by them to any third party

Types of Commercial Vehicle Insurance


Passenger Carriage Vehicle Insurance


This insurance is especially suitable for vehicles that carry one or more passengers such as taxis, auto-rickshaws, school buses, private buses, etc.

Passenger vehicles, especially school buses and regular cabs, carry a large number of passengers daily.

The life and income of a significant part of India's population depend on these vehicles. In such a situation, in case of any untoward incident with these vehicles, commercial vehicle insurance works to compensate financially for the losses and losses caused by it.

Luggage vehicle insurance


This insurance is for such vehicles which carry goods from one place to another. This mainly includes trucks, tempos, and lorries.

Vehicles carrying goods are often much larger and are more prone to accidents. Commercial vehicle insurance not only covers the damage caused to another person due to these vehicles but also works to protect the owner-driver and the vehicle at the time of damage to the vehicle due to an accident or natural causes. Is.

If your business is to transport goods from one place to another through trucks, then commercial vehicle insurance also gives financial benefits to the goods loaded in your vehicle in return for damage caused by any accident, fire, natural calamity, etc.

Insurance for other and special vehicles


Apart from cabs, taxis, trucks, and buses, many such vehicles are used for trade or in business. This includes certain vehicles such as vehicles used for various agricultural purposes and vehicles used in excavation and construction work.

Commercial vehicle insurance works to provide financial compensation to the driver and the owner of the vehicle in return for any damage or loss caused to these vehicles.

Considering the cost and size of these vehicles, commercial vehicle insurance is the best way to cover your losses. With the help of this, you can not only reduce the risk to yourself but also can avoid financial loss to yourself due to any untoward incident.

What is covered in commercial vehicle insurance?


Accident
This insurance covers damages caused to commercial vehicles due to an accident
Theft
Loss due to theft of a commercial vehicle or damage to the vehicle is covered in this insurance.
Fire
This vehicle insurance covers the damage caused to the commercial vehicle due to fire.
Natural calamity
The damage caused to the commercial vehicle due to any natural calamity is covered in this insurance.
Personal accident
The cover is provided for injuries to the driver in a commercial vehicle accident or the event of death.
Third party damage
This insurance covers damage caused to a third party due to a commercial vehicle. while pulling a damaged vehicle The damage caused in a commercial vehicle while pulling a damaged vehicle is also covered by this vehicle insurance.

What is not covered?

It is also very important to know what is not covered in your commercial vehicle insurance policy so that you do not face any kind of surprise or problem when you make a claim. Here are some such situations:

Self-loss for third party policyholders
If you have taken your third-party liability-only policy, then in this case your damage to your vehicle is not covered.
Driving under the influence of alcohol or without a license
If the owner or driver of the claimed vehicle is drunk or driving the vehicle without a proper license, then the cover will not be available.
Willful carelessness
Damage caused to the vehicle by the driver due to negligence (eg, damage caused by the driver driving the vehicle in flood conditions) will not be covered by this insurance.
Damage caused by any other cause
Any damage that is not caused by an accident or natural cause, such as loss in business or loss in the market, etc., cannot be covered by commercial vehicle insurance.
