LIC Bima Jyoti Plan

LIC: What is Bima Jyoti Plan, Eligibility, Features, Benefits, Surrender Value, Riders, Grace Period

In today's era, interest rates on deposits or other investments are decreasing. In such a situation, our country's largest insurance company LIC has launched a new insurance policy named Bima Jyoti. In which you will get guaranteed returns with fixed income.

Let us know what is LIC Bima Jyoti plan (plan no. 860) is? Its benefits, features, eligibility, and more

LIC: What is Bima Jyoti Plan?

LIC Bima Jyoti Plan is a non-linked, non-participating, individual guaranteed savings plan. This plan also protects along with savings. It is an endowment life insurance plan. This insurance pays the Sum Assured to the beneficiary on the death of the policyholder due to any reason. If the insured survives the policy term, he/she is paid the guaranteed amount.

LIC: Bima Jyoti Plan Eligibility

Insurance Name:- LIC Bima Jyoti Plan (Plan No. 860) Age of Entry:- Minimum:- 90 Days
Maximum:- 60 years
Coverage (Basic Sum Assured):- Minimum:- Rs. 1,00,000
Maximum:- No Limit (As per Board Approval)
Premium Paying Term:- At least 5 years
Policy Term:- 15 to 20 years
Maturity Age:- Minimum: 18 Years
Maximum: 75 Years

Features of LIC Bima Jyoti Plan
1.In this Bima Jyoti plan, you get the option to pay premiums monthly, quarterly, half-yearly, and annually.
2.This insurance is a combination of savings and protection.
3.In this insurance, the insured can avail of the loan after paying the premium for two years.
4.You can buy this insurance both online and offline.
5.The policy term in this LIC Bima Jyoti plan varies from 15 to 20 years. For a tenure of 15 years, you have to pay a premium for 10 years. And the same if you take a policy with a term of 20 years, then you need to pay for 15 years.
6.If you are not satisfied with this insurance plan, then you get a free look period of 15 days.

Benefits of LIC Bima Jyoti Plan

Death benefit
If the insured dies during the policy term, the Sum Assured is paid to the beneficiary/nominee of the insured. If the life insured dies before the date of commencement of risk, the premiums paid excluding taxes, additional premium, and rider premium (if any) are paid. If the policyholder dies after the date of commencement of risk then the Sum Assured along with the Sum Assured is paid out. Maturity benefit If the insured survives till the completion of the policy term, he is paid the Guaranteed Sum Assured along with the Maturity Sum Assured. The Sum Assured at the time of maturity will be equal to the Basic Sum Assured.
Additional Guaranteed Benefits
Guaranteed Additions @ Rs.50 per thousand Basic Sum Assured will be added to the policy at the end of each policy year.
Tax benefits
Any premium you pay for this policy is exempt under section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
Riders of LIC Bima Jyoti Plan
With this insurance policy, you get the option of 5 types of riders. Which the insured can add to his insurance plan by paying an additional premium amount. Accidental Death and Disability Benefit Rider accidental benefit rider New Term Assurance Rider New Critical Illness Benefit Rider Premium Waiver Benefit Rider
Grace period
There is a grace period of 30 days for quarterly, half-yearly, and annual premium payments. Same if you pay a monthly premium then you get 15 days off. If the premium is not paid before the expiry date of the grace period, the policy will terminate.
Surrender benefit
This insurance plan provides an option to surrender the policy at any time after the policy has been in force for two consecutive years. In such situations, the insurer pays the higher special surrender value or the surrender value equal to the guaranteed surrender value.
Revival
If the premium is not paid within the grace period, the policy terminates. LIC Bima Jyoti provides the option to revive the policy within 5 consecutive years. This means from the date of your first unpaid premium and it can be revived only before the maturity date.
