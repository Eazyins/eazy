What is Car Insurance?

What is Car Insurance? How does it work? and its benefits. What are car insurance and Benefits?

If you have bought a car for yourself, then you must know what is car insurance? If you are thinking of buying a new car, then you must know about car insurance. Car insurance provides you with financial support in case of an accident with your car.

Let us know in detail what is car insurance? How does it work, what is covered and what is not covered in car insurance, and what are its advantages or benefits?

What is Car Insurance?

Car Insurance The insurance company provides financial assistance to you in case of damage to your car in an accident. This type of insurance is also known as a package of motor insurance.

It is covered in case of theft of your car and damage to the insured, the insured car, and a third person or his property in an accident and the insurance company provides financial assistance.

How does car insurance work?

Car insurance is a contract between the insurance company and the car owner. Under which the car owner pays the premium (according to the plan of the insurance company) to the insurance company.

In which the insurance company provides you financial assistance in case of loss or loss of the insured car.
Most of the insurance companies in India keep partnerships with car companies. And when a person buys a new car, it gives him car insurance under certain offers.

Types of car insurance

There are mainly two types of car insurance available in India. Let us know in detail about this car insurance-

Third-party car insurance

Third-party car insurance covers damages caused to a third-party person and his property. For example, in an accident involving the death of a third party person, his death and the loss of property of the third party person.

In India, third-party insurance is mandatory for all types of road vehicles under the Motor Vehicles Act. As a car owner, you should also have third-party insurance

Comprehensive Car Insurance Comprehensive car insurance covers the death/injury of the driver of the car, passengers, and damage caused by the car to a third party vehicle, or property.
This insurance covers the third party cover as well as the car insured. It covers the expenses of both the insured car and third-party damages.

What is covered in car insurance?

Damage to Third Party Property: The insured car is paid in case of an accident with any other vehicle, in case of damage to the third party vehicle, and death/injury of the third party person.
Damage to your car: Damage caused to your car due to theft, accident, or natural calamities is covered.
Bodily injury: Injury to a driver, passengers, and third party person is covered in an accident.
Car Damage: Covers damage to your car and a third-party person's vehicle in an accident.
Death: Payable in the event of the death of the driver and passenger in the car in an accident.

What is not covered in car insurance?

Accidents caused by driving under the influence of alcohol or other intoxicants are not covered.
Accidents caused by driving a car without a driving license are not covered.
Car damage caused by an intentional accident is not covered.
Losses due to the aging of the car are not covered.
Damage caused by your car's oil leak is not covered.
Loss due to non-compliance with the terms of the insurance company is not covered.
If you sell your car to someone and the insurance of that car has not been transferred. If there is any loss in an accident then it is not covered.

Car insurance benefits

Personal Accident: Financial assistance is provided to a third party person in case of injury, death, or damage to his vehicle. Apart from third parties, many of you also get personal accident cover, under which you are covered in the event of your injury, death, or disability.
No Claim Bonus (NCB): Bonus is given when a person does not make any claim within the insurance period after taking the insurance. Which is known as the No Claim Bonus. This bonus helps in reducing your premium while renewing the policy.
Damage cover: Financially assisted in damages caused due to theft, accident, fire, terrorist activities, riots, explosions, and natural calamities.
Garage network: Many insurance companies have a large network of garages. Which is spread everywhere. So that you can get your car serviced anywhere.
Online Car Insurance: Nowadays it is the age of technology, taking advantage of this, many companies sell their insurance policies online. You too can buy online by choosing the right insurance policy for you.
Optional Add-ons: You can take optional add-ons to your car insurance as per your choice. Like zero depreciation, roadside assistance, replacement cover, etc. This add on is your safety
