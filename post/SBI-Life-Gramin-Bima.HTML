SBI Life Gramin Bima

SBI Life: What is Gramin Bima, Features, Benefits, Eligibility, Documents

Many people in India today are not aware of insurance. Because most of the population in India is still economically backward. Today more than half of the population in India lives in small villages. SBI Life Gramin Bima Yojana has been started by SBI Life to provide financial and life security to the people living in these villages.

Let us know in detail about SBI Life: What is Rural Insurance?

SBI Life: What is Gramin Bima Plan?

This insurance is specially designed for the poor people of the society. So that life insurance can be made available to the people of backward classes at a low cost. So that they can be helped financially or financially. SBI Life Gramin Bima is an individual, non-linked, non-participating micro-life insurance, pure risk premium product. This insurance objective is to provide cheap insurance to the economically backward and poor people in society. To provide financial security to the insured's family during any eventuality.

SBI Life: Grameen Bima Highlights


Insurance Policy Name: SBI Life: Grameen Bima
Age of Entry: Minimum: 18 Years
Maximum: 50 years
Premium Payment Mode: One Time Payment
Single-Premium Amount: Minimum Premium: Rs.300
Maximum Premium: Rs.2000
(The premium amount will be in multiples of Rs.100)
Basic Sum Assured: Minimum: Rs.10,000
Maximum: Rs 50,000
Insurance term: 5 years
Official Website: www.sbilife.co.in

SBI Life: Features of Rural Insurance

Free look period
In this insurance, the insured gets a free look period of 15 days. If you are not satisfied with this insurance plan, then you can close your insurance within 15 days.
Cheap life insurance
This SBI life term insurance plan offers benefits at an affordable premium. In which you need to make a one-time payment ranging from Rs.300 to Rs.2000. And the term of this insurance is 5 years.
Simple application process
This insurance has a simple application process with the required documents.
Financial security
This insurance provides financial protection to the beneficiary in the event of the death of the insured. So that after the death of the insured, his family does not have to face financial difficulties.

SBI Life: Benefits of Rural Insurance

Death benefit
After taking the insurance, if the insured dies during the term of insurance, the sum assured is paid to the beneficiary or nominee of the insured.
Insurance coverage
Age Basic Sum Assured
18 – 39 ,60 times the total premium paid
40 – 44 ,40 times the total premium paid
45 – 50 ,25 times the total premium paid
Tax Benefits
Your premium paid in this is eligible for tax exemption under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act 1961. And death benefits are eligible for tax deduction under section 10(10D) of the Income Tax Act 1961.
Maturity benefit
There is no maturity benefit of any kind in this insurance plan.
Surrender benefit
In the SBI Life Gramin Bima plan, the policyholder is given the option to surrender the policy, which will be available from the second year onwards.
This surrender value will be as follows:
Surrender Value = Single Premium x 50% x Policy Remaining Term (in months) / Total Term (in months)
Note:- No surrender benefit is given in the last year of this policy.

Required Documents
Identity Proof: Passport, Voter ID Identity Card.
Address Proof: Electricity Bill, Telephone Bill, Bank Account Statement, Aadhar Card, Passport, Voter ID Card.
Age Proof: Driving License, School/College Certificate, Passport, PAN Card, Birth Certificate.
