What is LIC New Endowment Plan?

LIC New Endowment Plan is a non-linked life insurance policy that offers guaranteed returns and bonuses. The policy offers an excellent bandwidth at the choice of the policy term. The policy term can be chosen from 12 to 35 years. It also has an extended age limit in which anyone between the age group of 8-55 years can avail of this plan and can be continued till 75 years. Entitled to a regular premium payment for the entire term of the policy. If an insured survives till the policy term, then he/she can get the maturity benefit i.e. Sum Assured along with Simple Reversionary Bonus + Final Additional Bonus is paid to the insured

Features of LIC New Endowment Plan

1.It is a participating traditional endowment plan.
2.It also provides surrender benefits and loan facilities in endowment plans.
3.This life insurance plan guarantees assured returns and bonuses.
4.On survival of the insured till the end of the policy term, the maturity benefit is paid to the policyholder.
5.The death benefit is paid to the beneficiary on the death of the insured within the policy term.
6.The maximum maturity age of this life insurance is 75 years.
7.The premiums for this policy can be paid in yearly, half-yearly, quarterly, and monthly modes.
8.LIC New Endowment Plan offers additional coverage in the form of an optional Accidental Death and Disability Benefit Rider. For which the policyholder will have to pay an additional premium.
9.The entry age of this plan ranges from a minimum of 8 years to a maximum of 55 years.
10.The minimum sum assured in this policy is one lakh.

Benefits of LIC New Endowment Plan

Death benefit
In case of death of the insured during the term of this policy, the beneficiary of the insured receives the death benefit along with a Simple Reversionary Bonus or accrued bonus as the total sum assured on death and the term of the policy.
Maturity benefit
If the policyholder survives the entire term of the policy, he/she will receive Basic Sum Assured along with accrued bonus as maturity benefit at the time of maturity of the policy and the term of the policy will end.
Profit-sharing
LIC New Endowment Plan participates in the company's profits and the policy collects Simple Reversionary Bonus and Final Additional Bonus which is declared based on the experience of the company. This benefit is offered to the insured as a death benefit or a simple reversionary bonus in case of maturity benefit provided the policy has completed a certain minimum number of years.

Riders of LIC New Endowment Plan
The following types of riders are available in LIC New Endowment Plan:
Accidental death benefit
Disability benefit
Grace period
There is a grace period of 30 days for quarterly, half-yearly, and annual premium payments. Same if you pay a monthly premium then you get 15 days off. If the premium is not paid before the expiry date of the grace period, the policy will terminate.
Surrender benefit
This insurance plan provides an option to surrender the policy at any time after the policy has been in force for two consecutive years. In such situations, the insurer pays the higher special surrender value or the surrender value equal to the guaranteed surrender value.
Revival
If the premium is not paid within the grace period, the policy terminates. LIC New Endowment provides the option to revive the policy within 5 consecutive years. This means from the date of your first unpaid premium and it can be revived only before the maturity date.
Free look period
If you are not happy with the coverage and terms and conditions of the policy, you have the option to cancel the policy within 15 days of receipt of the policy document, provided no claim has been made
