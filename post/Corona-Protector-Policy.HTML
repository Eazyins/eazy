Amid this corona pandemic, it is necessary to buy the right health insurance to cover the expenses of the disease. Which can help keep you financially secure. And what is covered in this insurance?

What is Corona Rakshak Policy?

Corona Rakshak policy is a health insurance policy. This policy was launched by the Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (IRDAI). This plan covers the cost of coronavirus treatment to the insured. Helps to deal with this pandemic. If the insured is found to be Kovid positive, he is paid a lump sum amount equal to 100% of the sum insured. This amount will be paid when the insured is hospitalized for 72 hours or more. And the treatment of Kovid should be done from the center authorized by the government. It is an individual, non-linked, non-participating, health insurance, pure risk premium product.

Features of Corona Rakshak Policy

All the features and coverage benefits listed in this COVID 19 health insurance plan are the same for all insurance providers. Let us have a look at their features: The minimum cover (sum insured) available in this Corona Rakshak policy is between Rs 50,000 and a maximum of Rs 2.5 lakh.
In case the insured is found to be COVID-19 positive, a lump sum amount equal to 100% of the sum insured is paid. This is a profit-based plan.
You are not required to undergo pre-medical screening before purchasing this insurance policy.
The tenure of this insurance plan is 3.5 months, 6.5 months, and 9.5 months.
In this, the sum insured can be availed on an individual basis.
The premium amount of Corona Rakshak policy is eligible for TEX exemption under section 80D.
The minimum waiting period of this insurance plan is 15 days.
The COVID-19 claim is settled within 30 days to 45 days from the date of receipt of the document.

What is covered in Corona Rakshak's policy?

Once the insured gets a COVID-19 positive report from a government authorized center, he can claim the following expenses as mentioned here:
Hospital Expenses: Room rent, cost of PPE kits, masks, gloves, nursing charges, ventilator charges, oxygen, boarding charges, diagnostic tests, etc. are provided.
Home Treatment Cover: Sum Insured can be used for the treatment of COVID-19 at home for a maximum period of 14 days.
Ambulance Expenses: Ambulance expenses for commuting to and from the hospital are covered.
ICU Cover: Covers the cost of ICU/ICCU expenses.
AYUSH cover: Sum insured can also be used for COVID-19 AYUSH treatment expenses.

What is not covered in Corona Rakshak's policy?

You cannot claim expenses incurred on diagnostic procedures and non-emergent expenses for existing treatment or diagnosis Any claim made during the waiting period will not be valid Claims for COVID-19 tests done at centers other than those recognized by the government are not accepted Your claim is not accepted if the insured is found positive while traveling through a country banned by the Government of India.

COVID Coverage- One of the major benefits is that it saves you from exorbitant hospitalization bills which are a concern for many COVID-19 affected patients.
100% Payout- Policyholder receives 100% lump sum payout on a positive diagnosis of Coronavirus
Eligibility Criteria – Individuals in the age group of 18 years to 65 years can buy this policy
Lump-sum payment – ​​One of the major benefits is that the insurer pays a lump sum amount irrespective of the actual hospitalization expenses incurred on the COVID-19 treatment
Standard Coverage Benefits – Policy benefits are standard for all insurers
