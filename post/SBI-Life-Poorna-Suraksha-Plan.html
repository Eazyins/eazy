SBI Life Poorna Suraksha Plan

SBI Life: What is Purna Suraksha Plan, Features, Benefits, Life Stage Rebalancing, Critical Illness Cover, Insurance Premium

Your needs change with age, so you must have a financial plan for this. The future is uncertain but preparation for it can be done from today. With SBI Poorna Suraksha Insurance Plan, you can cover more effectively. This plan provides both life and health insurance.

SBI Life: What is Complete Protection Life Insurance?

SBI life insurance Complete protection Life insurance is a traditional term life insurance that comes with critical illness insurance. That is, in this plan you get the benefit of both life and health insurance. It is a non-participating, non-linked term insurance, pure risk premium plan. This plan auto-rebalances your life insurance and critical illness cover every year. This auto rebalance happens on the date on which you have purchased the insurance. This insurance provides you with comprehensive protection in case of death or critical illness.

Features of SBI Complete Protection Life Insurance

Comprehensive Coverage: This insurance provides you coverage in case of death or critical illness.
Coverage for 36 types of critical illnesses: Coverage for 36 types of critical illnesses is provided in this plan. For example, cancer, heart attack, brain tumor, kidney failure, etc.
Unique Life Stage Rebalancing: 'Lifestage Re-balancing' feature rebalances Life cover and Critical Illness (CI) cover.
Waiver of Premium: Another unique feature of this plan is that it provides a premium waiver on the diagnosis of any of the listed critical illnesses.
Fixed Premium: In this plan, your premium remains the same throughout the term. Irrespective of the increase in age of the insured or an increase in critical illness coverage, the premium amount remains the same throughout the policy term.

Benefits of SBI Complete Protection Life Insurance Life cover: In case of death of the insured due to any reason in the future, the life cover sum assured is paid to the beneficiary of the insured under this plan.
Life Stage Rebalancing: This is one of the most important benefits of the SBI Life Purna Suraksha plan. Life Stage Rebalancing is a unique feature, which helps in balancing the coverage between life and critical illness.
Premium Waiver Benefit: All future premiums for this insurance plan are waived off on diagnosis of critical illness. After the implementation of this benefit, the Life Stage Rebalancing is over and thereafter the Life Cover Sum Assured continues.
Fixed Premium Benefit: The premium amount remains the same throughout the term of the policy irrespective of the age of the insured or aggravation of the critical illness.
Critical Illness Benefit: This plan is covered in the case of critical illness. Critical Illness Sum Assured is paid. Note that the Critical Illness Benefit will be paid after surviving 14 days from the date of the first diagnosis.
Tax Benefits: In this plan, the policyholder can avail of tax benefits under Section 80C and Section 80D of the Income Tax Act, 1961.

What is Life Stage Rebalancing in this plan?

Life stage rebalancing is the specialty of this plan, which rebalances the coverage between life insurance and critical illness. Life Insurance and Critical Illness cover are divided in the ratio of 80:20 when the Sum Assured of this plan starts.
After that, as the year's pass, the Critical Illness Sum Assured increases. And in the same way, your life cover sum assured will get reduced. This change in Sum Assured will happen from the date on which the policy is purchased. The Total Sum Assured (Life Cover Sum Assured + Critical Illness Sum Assured) remains the same throughout the policy term.

What is covered in critical illness?
In this insurance, along with life cover, coverage for critical illness is also provided. If the covered person is diagnosed with any kind of critical illness then he can take the benefit of Critical Illness Sum Assured.
But you can take advantage of this critical illness cover only once. This cover expires after payment. There is a waiting period of 90 days from the date of commencement of the policy to get the payment of this cover. The insured needs to be alive for at least 14 days from the date of onset of critical illness to get paid for the covered illness. Under this insurance, it has been told that in the case of critical illness, 36 types of critical diseases are covered.
