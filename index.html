What is Commercial vehicle insurance

This motor insurance policy is for business vehicles and can be customized to suit your needs. Commercial vehicle insurance covers any damage and loss caused to or caused by commercial vehicles. This includes damage to vehicles due to an accident, sabotage, natural causes, and fire. All businesses must buy commercial vehicle insurance for all their commercial vehicles such as auto-rickshaws, cabs, school buses, and trucks.
